Praslin,Seychelles

Seychelles is a cluster of charming islands found in the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, off East Africa. While it’s true that the archipelago serves as a tropical, luxury honeymoon hotspot, this island nation also proves that it’s much more than that with its gorgeous landscapes, abundant nature, and rich culture. The waters here show off a sensational spectrum of blues, greens and everything in between. You’ll often find yourself reluctant to get out of the water and leave the beach.



Hotels in Praslin,Seychelles

Rosemary's Guesthouse

Located in Praslin, Rosemary's Guesthouse offers beachfront accommodations a few steps from Anse Volbert Beach and has facilities like a garden and barbecue facilities. Every room comes with a balcony with views of the sea.At the guesthouse, the rooms come with a patio with a garden view.

Hotel L'Archipel

Located along the white and sandy Anse Gouvernement Beach, Hotel L’Archipel features a swimming pool and tropical garden. It also has 2 restaurants.Guests can relax by the pool or the beach. Beachside restaurant, La Gigolette, offers international breakfast, lunch and a la carte seafood for dinner.

Pirogue Lodge

Pirogue Lodge is located next to Cote D’Or Beach.The property features car rental and shuttle services.The property is a 15-minute drive to Baie Ste Anne Jetty. It is a 30-minute drive from Amitie Airport.Rooms come with a balcony, satellite TV and mini-bar. Free toiletries and hairdryer are provided in an private bathroom.

Colibri Hotel

Surrounded by tropical gardens, Colibri offers spacious chalets with a balcony or terrace with views of Baie Ste Anne. It features a seafront restaurant and an infinity pool.The staff at Colibri can arrange snorkeling, fishing, and boating trips. The hotel is located only 5 minutes’ walk from the inter-island ferry jetty.
